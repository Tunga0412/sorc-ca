An independent research collaborative producing methodologically rigorous work using publicly available data. We prioritise bringing junior researchers into substantive contributory roles.

Works tagged SORC are produced under the collaborative. Select Member Works are peer-reviewed publications by SORC members conducted through other research settings.

Contact us at info@sorc.ca
